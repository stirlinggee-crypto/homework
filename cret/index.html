Cretaceous Period

This was the era of famous dinosaurs like the Tyrannosaurus rex, Triceratops, and Velociraptor.


The first flowering plants (angiosperms) appeared and began to diversify rapidly.


The supercontinent Pangea continued to break apart, forming the early outlines of modern continents.


The period concluded with a mass extinction event, widely attributed to a large asteroid impact, which wiped out approximately 75% of all species, including all non-avian dinosaurs.
