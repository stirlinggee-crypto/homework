Ordovician Period

The Ordovician Period was the 2nd Period in the paleozoic era.


The oceans were busy with creatures like armored, jawless fish, tentacled cephalopods (relatives of squid), sea scorpions, and brachiopods (shell creatures).


The first simple plants, like mosses, began to grow on land, but they were tiny and needed to stay near water.


This began the first major ice age, freezing water into giant glaciers and making sea levels fall dramatically.
