Cambrian Period

This era saw the first appearance of animals with hard shells and exoskeletons, which makes it easier to form fossils.


Oxygen levels in the oceans increased Greatly, probably fueled by a large amount of photosynthesizing algae, which provided the energy needed for more complex and active animals.


This period is famous for the Cambrian Explosion, when many new types of animals suddenly appeared.


Animals started digging into the mud for the first time, which helped move oxygen into the ocean floor.
