Paleogene Period

During this time, early ancestors of whales were small, four-legged land animals that slowly adapted to living in the ocean, so once whales walked.


Since there weren't many large predators yet, giant flightless "Terror Birds" ruled the land. Some stood up to 10 feet tall with massive, bone-crushing beaks.


Early ancestors of modern animals like dogs, cats, horses, and even monkeys first appeared during this period.
